Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Laser Maze ("we", "our", or "the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Laser Maze does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All game progress (level completion, currency, cosmetic purchases, and lives) is stored locally on your device using standard app storage and is never sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. Google AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location information
  • App usage data

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Children's Privacy

Laser Maze is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The ads displayed in the App are configured to comply with applicable regulations.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data retention policy applicable to us. Game progress stored locally on your device can be deleted by uninstalling the App.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data for us to provide, modify, or delete. To manage data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's privacy settings and your device's advertising settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
